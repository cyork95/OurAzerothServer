Description
The Annals of Darrowshire, through my magic, has gained pages at its end, and now it extends its history beyond the battle. One of the extra passages tells of another Redpath, Carlin, who survived Joseph's betrayal. Carlin's now in the Plaguelands to the east, at the Light's Hope Chapel.

Take the book and speak with him.

By wingsofscion (11,746 – 1·22·97) on 2008/07/11 (Patch 2.4.1)

The full quest chain for The Battle of Darrowshire:
1. Little Pamela
2. Pamela's Doll
3. Auntie Marlene
4. A Strange Historian
5. The Annals of Darrowshire
6. Brother Carlin
7a. Villains of Darrowshire
7b. Heroes of Darrowshire
8. Marauders of Darrowshire
9. Return to Chromie
10. The Battle of Darrowshire
11. Hidden Treasures
Villains and Heroes do both need to be completed in order to get Marauders. However, Defenders of Darrowshire (the Uncle Carlin chain) does not need to be completed in order to get to The Battle of Darrowshire.
