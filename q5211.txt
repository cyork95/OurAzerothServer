Description
Many of the defenders fell at the battle of Darrowshire. And many roam again as the ghastly servants of the Scourge! Find these poor souls and end their misery!

Hunt cannibal ghouls, gibbering ghouls and diseased flayers. Many of these fiends were once the good folk of Darrowshire and destroying their forms now will set their souls free.

You will find them in Plaguewood and Corin's Crossing, and in other Scourge strongholds of Eastern Plaguelands.

I used the location above (68, 41) on my Warrior, and it worked really well. Just on the first sweep through I got ten down, and it honestly could have been more if I widened my search area. Personally, I think this quest is great, especially for First Aid users. The Runecloth drop rate here is great. I started with 2 Heavy Runecloth Bandages, ended with 12, and that's with my use of them. I didn't have to eat a single bit of food.

By Yhllethil (11,126 – 1·7·18) on 2024/07/16 (Classic)

The ghouls can spawn in place of other mobs in the area.
If you (and you will) come across long stretches without seeing them you should just kill the mobs there so they can respawn as a ghoul.

The Scourge Guards for example were always good targets for me.

A macro that I can recommend is this one:


/target Cannibal ghoul
/target Diseased flayer
/target Gibbering ghoul
/script SetRaidTarget("target",4)
That way you can just run around and spam this macro and the target will basically blink as you mark & unmark them rapidly.
I personally bound it to scrollwheel, but I can also recommend the addon Unitscan for it as you can easily add & remove units by typing "/unitscan NAMEOFMOB" and it will notify you like any other rarescanner or npcscan addon.

Enjoy!
