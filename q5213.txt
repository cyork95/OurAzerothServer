By 85347 (974 – 8) on 2008/01/20 (Patch 2.3.2)

I managed to pull this quest off solo as a lvl70 rogue thanks to this post. Start by entering the side entrance and then opening the service entrance gate, pass through it and stealth. Continue up to the gauntlet gate and distract the stealth detecting mobs when needed. After you've opened the second gauntlet gate you'll see the boss you want to kill straight ahead. The door behind her will open when she dies and the chest you're looking for may be inside. If not, you can have a shot at Nerub'enkan located to your left after the second gauntlet gate. But keep in mind that this fight will be alot harder because he's melee and he spawns two adds, at least he did for me.

I had to use 2x evasion and a healing pot to survive even though I'm almost fully epic geared. Now, there's a risk that the chest is not located inside that place either and if so you'll have to reset the instance and try again (unless you feel like going for the third boss, which I never tried myself). MoD is strongly recommended, camouflage helps as well against the gargoyles. And remember that there's stealth detecting mobs in there that's also cloaked, called "Eye of Naxxramas", which you can only hope you won't encounter. It's also worth noting that the chest you're looking for is not trackable with the dwarf racial "find treasure".

By Zelzaikan (5,054 – 1·8·15) on 2025/02/18 (Classic)

This quest is the second quest in the chain. in order to be eligible to pick up this quest you will need to complete the quest  The Flesh Does Not Lie. You can do that quest in either side of Stratholme although the Undead side is especially recommended. This quest will have you enter Stratholme Undead side and loot a box containing the data. This box is tiny and can spawn in any one of the three Ziggurats in the Gauntlet area. Each Ziggurat has 1 boss at the entrance, Nerub'Enkan, Baroness Anastari, and Maleki. Inside of the Ziggurats there will also be 5 Trash Mobs. When you have collected the data, turn in the quest to Betina Bigglezink at 81.4 59.6 Lights Hope Chapel, Eastern Plaguelands. You can choose between 2 different trinkets,  Seal of the Dawn which gives +81 Attack Power against Undead mobs and  Rune of the Dawn which gives +48 spellpower against Undead mobs. This trinket replaces your Argent Dawn Commission and can be recreated by Betina Bigglezink.

From AllakhazamBy jugdick on 2007/12/18 (Patch 2.3.0)

Subject: "Solo'd - lvl 70 fury warrior"
Solo'd this quest as a lvl 70 fury warrior, no pots, quite a few deaths. I entered Strat from the side entrance - UD side. Watch aggro as you make your way to the gate on your right. You'll come to a T, take a right to the next gate. This will head towards the 3 Ziggurats where you will find 3 bosses - Baroness Anastari, Nerub'enkan or Maleki the Pallid.

The fastest way I found to complete this quest was to head towards Baroness Anastari (straight ahead from the second gate). Check out xxxstonie's map below and Anastari will be at the Zig to the East. You can either pick mobs off one or two at a time for a slow going stroll to Anastari, OR...being mindfull of those annoying gargoyles, you can time it right and get to Anastari without aggroing, however, it is difficult. I wiped a couple times figuring it out after finding out how close I could get to mobs without aggroing.

Once you make it to Anastari, full out dps'ing killed her with 40% of my health remaining. I resisted most spells easily with 0 shadow resistance. The fight did not require me to use any cooldowns (ie. retaliation, recklessness). After she's dead, Zig doors open up. 5 non-elite mobs inside, easy as pie. If the tiny chest with the Scourge Data is not in the Zig, tough luck. Try again. Exit instance, reset, repeat steps above. Took me 6 tries - tons of loot, 17g repair bill. Pathetic.....warrior solo'ing sucks!
