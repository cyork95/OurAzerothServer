By 364 (6,604 – 3·23·38) on 2006/09/18 (Patch 1.12.0)

Though this is a quest item, it's one a player is able to use.

If you equip this item in your trinket slot, it will drain both your mana and your hp until you either remove the item or you die. The number it drains both by is random, so sometimes it will only drain a low amount of hp/mana every second (100 of each), or it will drain a large amount of hp/mana (500 of each). Usually the numbers will fall inbetween the two extremes.

Although you are technically supposed to turn this in to complete the questline it is involved with, you might want to hold onto it for it's special lethal properties

When a character is killed by equipping this item, that character will not suffer any armor damage. This is useful if one needs to kill themself in order to speak with ghost characters, such as the ghost in BRD in order to get the Shadowforge Key quest.

Also, if you get stuck in a location that you cannot easily get out of and your hearthstone's cooldown is not up yet, you can use this item to kill yourself so that you can be sent to the nearest graveyard.

Personally, if you are not going to be benefited by any of the rewards, experience, or money, you might want to hold onto this unique, suicidal item instead of turning it in.
