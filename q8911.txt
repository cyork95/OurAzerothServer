The items you should collect before starting the quest chain

Obviously you need to collect the D1 set of your class, the Helm and the Chest should be the last two items on your agenda, you can complete the whole quest chain even if you dont have them (they are the last reward for your troubles), Some items can be bought from the AH (the belt, the bracer and the gauntlets).
You need 180 gold (slightly less if you are revered or exalted with the argent dawn)
a Delicate Arcanite Converter,
4 Greater Eternal Essence,
10 Stonescale Oil,
a few barrels of Goblin Rocket fuel (it depends, on how good you are with AoE pulling, if you are very good you wont need any),
a Fel Elemental Rod from Vi'el he sells it for 40 gold (this 40g is included in the 180gold above,
3 Dark Iron Bar,
20 Enchanted Leather,
3 Mooncloth,
4 Cured Rugged Hide,
4 Dark Rune,
8 Large Brilliant Shard,
a Flask of Supreme Power
The quest Three Kings of Flame will send you to Silithus to kill one of the The Duke of Cynders, to summon him you need a
1 Scroll: Signet of Beckoning (the fire one), to create a Signet of Beckoning: Fire
one set of the Twilight Trappings Set,
1 Medallion of Station
Most probably you wont find neither the Scroll: Signet of Beckoning nor the Signet of Beckoning: Fire on AH. There are two ways to work around this problem.
The first way (tho less likely to achieve) is to start the quest The Twilight Mystery, do the follow ups (The Deserter, The Twilight Lexicon) and you should be able to see a new quest, True Believers. This last quest is repeatable (Still Believing), by handing in several pages of Encrypted Twilight Text you get a Decoded True Believer Clippings in your mail in 10-12 hours, these bags have a very-very low chance to drop the Scroll: Create Signet of Beckoning.
The other way is to collect 3 sets of the Twilight Trappings Set, summon a Templar at the Lesser Windstones and collect 3 Abyssal Crests, Hand them in with a Large Brilliant Shard for the quest Dukes of the Council. Collect a 4th set of the Twilight Trappings, and summon a Duke at a Windstone. You will have 25% chance that the Duke you will summon is the one you need.
